# SALES CUSTOMER INSIGHT DASHBOARD PROJECT REPORT

## 1. Introduction

The Sales Customer Insight Dashboard project is developed to analyze business sales performance, customer behavior, financial trends, and customer service operations using Microsoft Excel. The project transforms raw business data into meaningful insights through interactive dashboards, visual reports, and KPI monitoring systems.

The dashboard helps organizations monitor:

* Sales performance
* Revenue trends
* Product demand
* Customer satisfaction
* Customer support activities
* Operational efficiency

This project demonstrates the practical implementation of business analytics and data visualization techniques using Excel-based dashboard solutions.

# 8. Order Fulfillment Dashboard

## Purpose

The Order Fulfillment Dashboard monitors operational sales performance and product demand trends.

## Key Performance Indicators

* Total Orders: 794
* Total Revenue: ₹4,38,268
* Average Revenue: ₹551.97
* Average Discount Given: 47%

## Dashboard Components

### Most Ordered Products

Displays the products with the highest demand:

* Crispy Chole Pizzabun
* Paneer Tikka Pizzabun
* Medium Crispy Chole
* Minty Pizzabun

### Daily Sales Trend

Shows daily order fluctuations and sales activity over time.

### Revenue by Product

Displays product-wise revenue contribution.

### Daily Revenue Trend

Tracks day-wise revenue generation.

### Order Type Analysis

Analyzes:

* Online Orders
* Physical Visit Orders

## Key Insights

* Crispy Chole Pizzabun generated the highest order volume.
* Online orders contributed significantly to sales revenue.
* Revenue patterns closely followed sales trends.
* Discount strategies influenced customer purchases.

# 9. Finance Dashboard

## Purpose

The Finance Dashboard evaluates revenue generation and overall financial performance.

## Dashboard Components

### Total Sales by Day

Shows revenue generated each day.

### Average Sales by Day

Displays average transaction value trends.

### Actual vs Rounded Sales

Compares actual sales values with rounded sales figures.

### Sales Amount by Bucket

Categorizes revenue into:

* ₹300–₹500
* ₹500–₹700
* ₹700–₹900

### Sold Products by Bucket

Displays product sales distribution based on revenue categories.

### Product Sales vs Average Price

Compares product revenue with average product pricing.

## Key Insights

* Premium products generated maximum revenue.
* High-value sales buckets contributed significantly to overall revenue.
* Revenue remained stable across multiple periods.
* Average pricing supported consistent business growth.
